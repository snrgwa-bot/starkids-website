Why Families Love StarKids

📋

Daily Tasks

Create age-appropriate chores with star values. Kids see their daily to-do list and check off completed tasks.

⭐

Earn Stars

Every completed task earns stars. Watch the points grow and celebrate progress with fun animations.

🏪

Reward Store

Set up rewards like screen time, treats, or special outings. Kids save up and redeem their hard-earned stars.

🔥

Streaks & Badges

Daily streaks keep kids motivated. Earn badges for milestones like completing 10 tasks or a 7-day streak.

🎯

Savings Goals

Set big goals and watch progress build over time. Teaches kids the value of saving and delayed gratification.

🔒

Parent Controls

PIN-protected parent mode for managing tasks, rewards, and settings. Kid mode keeps things simple and safe.
